Welcome! This is the home page. To open the Report Menu, you can press on the Report Tab.

You can add and view reports here. To add a report, press on the Add Report button.

This is the Add Report Menu. Once here, enter the required information and scroll to the bottom and press Save.

To enter Analyte information, you can press the Analyte button to toggle the Analyte fields.

If you want to view this tutorial again, press the tutorial button in the Settings tab.
